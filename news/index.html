MedAptus News

South Shore Hospital Automates Charge Capture and Management via MedAptus’ EHR Interoperability

Boston – August 9, 2016 – MedAptus® today announced that South Shore Hospital has implemented the company’s Pro Charge Capture solution for simplified inpatient encounter documentation. More than 60 clinicians from the Hospital Medicine Team access the Pro solution from within the facility’s MEDITECH EMR system. This unique capability from MedAptus enables efficient and accurate charge documentation as a provider simultaneously completes clinical documentation.

Cardiology Associates of North Mississippi has implemented Pro Charge solution

Boston – June 7, 2016 – MedAptus® today announced that Cardiology Associates of North Mississippi has implemented the company’s Pro Charge solution to improve physician documentation of professional charges for care delivered at North Mississippi Medical Center.

MedAptus First in KLAS Mobile Data Systems Rankings

Boston – February 3, 2016 – MedAptus® today announced that its Professional Charge Capture (Pro) solution has been named the Mobile Data Systems Category Leader by KLAS in its “2015/2016 Best in KLAS: Software & Services” report. The MedAptus Pro Charge Capture solution has now been recognized with this distinction for the past five out of six years. This year’s category-topping score of 90.5 is based on customer feedback across satisfaction areas including functionality, implementation and support.
