MedAptus Tops 2017 KLAS Rankings for Mobile Charge Capture

February 7, 2017 - Home / News

Boston – February 7, 2017 – MedAptus® today announced that its Professional Charge Capture (Pro) solution has been named the 2017 Category Leader in the Mobile Charge Capture segment in the “2017 Best in KLAS Awards: Software & Services” report. The company's top-ranking score of 94.3 is based on customer feedback across measures evaluating vendor performance data around areas touching overall satisfaction. This latest recognition marks the sixth time in the last seven years that MedAptus has earned this honor.

MedAptus' suite of revenue cycle enhancement solutions cover professional, technical, and infusion services coding and billing processes. The company's Pro Charge customers range in size from single-specialty group practices to large provider enterprises. These organizations consistently experience improved revenue, accelerated cash, better productivity and enhanced ICD-10 compliance as a result of adopting MedAptus.

"The last two years at MedAptus have been exciting ones, particularly as we have successfully responded to customer and market demand for better care coordination tools. Despite the focus we have put forth on novel patient assignment and team collaboration technology offerings, we have maintained a strong focus on delivering exceptional experiences for our revenue cycle customers,” according to Eugene Schneider, MedAptus president and CEO. “We are delighted to lead a category of worthy competitors and look forward to serving organizations in need of effective revenue enhancement technology for many years to come.”

MedAptus Pro Charge Capture is available on most mobile hardware platforms for busy physicians on-the-go; the solution is also interoperable with a number of leading EHR solutions from vendors including MEDITECH, Epic and Allscripts. This unique capability enables an efficient charge documentation process for providers who are only facing increasing administrative burden in a time when system leverage is key to most IT leaders.
