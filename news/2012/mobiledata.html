MedAptus First Again in KLAS Mobile Data Systems Rankings

January 26, 2012 - Home / News / 2012

Boston – January 26, 2012 – MedAptus® today announced that its Professional Intelligent Charge Capture solution has once again been named the Mobile Data Systems Category Leader by KLAS. MedAptus’ top-ranking score in the “2011 Best in KLAS Awards: Software & Services” report is based on positive customer feedback across 25 measures evaluating performance in areas such as product quality, implementation and support.

MedAptus provides comprehensive charge capture, charge management, and related analytical tools that simplify and streamline coding and billing processes. Faster, more accurate professional coding drives strong financial benefit, improves compliance adherence, and yields overall productivity gains. MedAptus’ flagship software offering, Professional Intelligent Charge Capture, received an 8.3 out of 9.0 product quality rating in the KLAS report, increasing its score from the prior year and its score differential over the next closest competitive offering.

“Of course we are very pleased to receive the top KLAS ranking once again in our category. Our consistently high KLAS scores and continued growth in our user base speak not only to the quality of our solutions and our service teams, but also to our ability to bring significant and immediate financial return to our customers,” said Larry Hagerty, MedAptus president. “Being able to favorably impact customer competitiveness is of particular importance today as many of our customers struggle with reimbursement pressures at the same time they are readying themselves for ICD-10. We help them address both issues.”

KLAS is a research firm specializing in monitoring and reporting the performance of healthcare vendors and works with executives from more than 4,500 hospitals and over 2,500 clinics. The mission of KLAS is to improve delivery by independently measuring vendor performance for the benefit of healthcare provider partners, consultants, investors and vendors.
