MedAptus Introduces Mobile Schedule App for Providers

September 24, 2012 - Home / News / 2012

Boston – September 24, 2012 – MedAptus®, the market leader for charge capture technologies, today announced the availability of its Mobile Schedule application for the Apple® iOS platform. This latest MedAptus offering leverages company’s iPhone® and iPad® platform expertise to provide convenient mobile access to physician schedules and pertinent patient details.

The development of the MedAptus Mobile Schedule solution grew out of increasing market demand for access to real-time schedule information by physicians who frequently travel between various treatment locations. With the increasing adoption of smartphone technology in healthcare, the availability of Mobile Schedule allows physicians on-the-move to use their time more effectively. Beyond providing secure and ready access to who is being seen next and where, the app’s drilldown functionality reveals relevant demographic details including reason for patient visit, referring provider, insurance carrier, and more. A robust search function and sort/filter capability enhances physician navigation.

“With Mobile Schedule we saw a nice opportunity to combine our expertise in both mobile development and data integration with a need for getting scheduling and census data out of legacy systems and into providers’ hands,” said Gene Schneider, executive vice president of operations at MedAptus. “We are always looking for ways to use technology to streamline the administrative burden of physicians.”

MedAptus is probably best known for its Pro and Facility Charge Capture applications, powerful and flexible solutions that have garnered widespread adoption for coding and billing process transformation and significant financial improvement. Clinical end-users of the suite benefit from embedded patient, visit and compliance intelligence at the point-of-care while administrative personnel enjoy enhanced charge review, reconciliation and reporting capabilities.
