MedAptus First in KLAS Mobile Data Systems Rankings

February 3, 2016 - Home / News

Boston – February 3, 2016 – MedAptus® today announced that its Professional Charge Capture (Pro) solution has been named the Mobile Data Systems Category Leader by KLAS in its “2015/2016 Best in KLAS: Software & Services” report. The MedAptus Pro Charge Capture solution has now been recognized with this distinction for the past five out of six years. This year’s category-topping score of 90.5 is based on customer feedback across satisfaction areas including functionality, implementation and support.

MedAptus’ flagship Charge Capture solution, Pro, enables provider groups to experience easy, streamlined ICD-10 coding as well as improved reconciliation and overall revenue cycle performance transparency thanks to a companion set of analytical tools. Pro customers cover the range of provider practices across enterprises such as hospital medicine departments, integrated delivery networks and academic medical centers.

“Our team is beyond thrilled to have again captured this distinction given how dedicated we are to providing our customers with the best all-around experience – intuitive functionality, demonstrable ROI, responsive support and innovative applications,” said Corey Richardson, senior vice president of sales and chief revenue officer for MedAptus. “And the best is yet to come for MedAptus customers. As we add our new Assign platform for optimized patient list creation and distribution, we take lessons from where we have delivered incredible value for our Pro Charge customers. We will absolutely provide the same unparalleled experience for groups interested in transforming the patient-physician assignment process into something rich and intelligent as opposed to a manual task offering zero quality insight.”

MedAptus Assign is a stand-alone platform that can easily integrate with any charge capture solution to achieve effortless rounding and reconciliation for inpatient-focused providers such as hospitalists.
