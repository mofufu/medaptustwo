MedAptus First in KLAS Mobile Data Systems Rankings

January 3, 2013 - Home / News / 2013

Boston – January 3, 2013 – MedAptus® today announced that its Professional Charge Capture (Pro) solution has again been named the Mobile Data Systems Category Leader by KLAS in its “2012 Best in KLAS Awards: Software & Services” report. The MedAptus Pro solution has maintained this leadership position for three years in a row. The company’s top-ranking category score of 93 is based on customer feedback across performance areas that include functionality, implementation and support.

MedAptus provides comprehensive charge capture, charge management, and related analytical tools that simplify and streamline physician coding and billing processes. Faster, more accurate professional coding drives strong financial benefit, improves compliance adherence, and yields overall productivity gains. MedAptus’ flagship software offering, Professional Charge Capture, received an 8.3 out of 9.0 product quality rating in the KLAS report with 100 percent of customers indicating they would buy the Pro solution again.

“Our team works very hard to deliver a highly satisfactory customer experience and I am very pleased to see their efforts recognized once again by this KLAS distinction. Increasing competition, expanded regulation, and changing reimbursement programs are all current realities affecting the healthcare industry. Our customers depend upon us to stay focused on high quality service and support to help them achieve ongoing improvements in critical business outcomes” said Larry Hagerty, MedAptus president. “The recognition from KLAS is a great way for MedAptus to start 2013. This year will be pivotal for many of our customers as they begin to prepare in earnest for ICD-10 and we look forward to working with them every step of the way.”
