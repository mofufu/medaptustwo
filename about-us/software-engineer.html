Careers - Sr. Software Engineer

*** MedAptus will not use recruiters for this role. Only actual applicants should apply.***

Job Title Sr. Software Engineer

Department Software Development

Location Raleigh, North Carolina

Company Overview

MedAptus products have been the gold standard when it comes to hospital and physician point-of-care revenue cycle management for years. Now MedAptus is expanding their product suite to include a new suite of products while continuing to build out more features of their product line.

Position Overview

MedAptus is seeking a full time software developer to join our development team in Raleigh, NC. We are looking for a candidate with expertise in back-end (Spring Framework, Hibernate, Java, SQL Server).

We are seeking a self-starting and self-motivated professional to assist us with building out the user interfaces of our core products. This individual will also assist in the development of these new product offerings. The ideal candidate must have a strong background in user interface development and a track record of accomplishing objectives on time in an Agile environment.

The primary responsibility of Sr. Software Engineer is to help MedAptus build its new suite of products while maintaining its dominance as the best point-of-care revenue cycle management tool in the healthcare market. The candidate must have strong skills needed to create and implement user interfaces that allow healthcare providers to spend more time caring for patients by reducing time needed for data entry. The developer will collaborate with the existing development team in the design and implementation of our cloud, mobile, and tablet product offerings. The developer must be results driven, agile, and have that creative gene. The developer will work with internal experts and end users to achieve the best experience possible.

Duties

  1. Produce software with exceptional quality in the estimated time frame
  2. Engage with internal and external resources to understand the end user needs
  3. Identify and present user interface options
  4. Research new technologies that allow for ever-increasing development efficiency
  5. Collaborate with the development team
  6. Employ a test driven development approach

Personal Specification

Work with product teams to understand and document the software requirements and design. This position, at times, will require the technical competency to create a specification so other developers can implement the solution in a high-quality fashion. The candidate will have strong problem solving skills and the ability to get to the core issue. This position requires an enthusiastic and self-motivated individual with a strong work ethic and excellent communication skills.

Required Education, Skills, Knowledge and Experience

  1. Bachelors’ degree in computer science or related field
  2. 5 years of professional software development experience
  3. Proven ability to deliver high quality code
  4. Ability to balance creativity with software engineering principles
  5. Ability to translate functional and usability requirements into a design and specification
  6. Excellent grasp of object-oriented analysis and design
  7. Experience working in an Agile team, delivering high-quality products
  8. Experience with a service-oriented architecture (SOA) a plus
  9. Experience with mobile software development a plus

Core Technical Skills

Java, J2SE, J2E

JavaScript (Sencha)
