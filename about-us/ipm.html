Implementation Project Manager – Boston, MA

Job Title: Implementation Project Manager – Boston, MA

Department: Client Services

Position Overview

The Project Manager will work with others within Client Services, including Technical Support, Integration, and Management, to successfully implement the MedAptus software for various healthcare provider organizations.

Essential Job Responsibilities

  1. Oversees and manages the full project lifecycle from the Sales to Services hand-off to the Go-Live event
  2. Coordinates various parties involved with the implementation of the MedAptus solution, including managing internal and external expectations and resources
  3. Creates and maintains a project plan for the Customer and internally
  4. Defines and measures deadlines and milestones
  5. Analyzes existing customer business flow to determine how the MedAptus solution will address their needs
  6. Works closely with integration team to translate customer requirements into HL7 strategy
  7. Establishes an understanding of customer business and technical infrastructures for proper translation to the MedAptus solution
  8. Tests software to ensure a smooth installation
  9. Performs training of the software in person at the customer site and remotely over the Internet utilizing web tools
  10. Troubleshoots issues and clearly reports them to the technical staff and development
  11. Ensures a timely and proper installation of all work items through diligent resource management
  12. Captures and reports new feature requests to development
  13. Identifies and communicates additional revenue opportunities to Account Management and Sales
  14. Creates, organizes and updates customer documentation as newer versions of software becomes available

Required Education, Skills, Knowledge and Experience

  1. BA/BS Degree, or equivalent
  2. 5+ years project management/implementation experience in a healthcare environment
  3. Well versed on the complex nature of large academic medical institutions
  4. Experienced in business process redesign resulting from implementation of healthcare technology solutions
  5. End-user software training experience required
  6. Must have excellent presentation skills
  7. Flexible to adapt to a dynamic and rapidly growing organization
  8. Ability to multitask and handle multiple concurrent projects under time constraints
  9. Experience in configuration management and associated documentation and certification processes
  10. Ability to follow-up and resolve issues in a timely manner to satisfy the client
  11. Outstanding communication, organization team and customer relationship skills
  12. System testing and quality assurance experience a plus
  13. Physician office information technology and healthcare information technology experience a must
  14. Knowledge of HL7, general interface capabilities, LAN/WAN, hardware, and peripherals a plus
  15. Must be able to travel 75%+

To apply, please email a cover letter describing your skills, employment goals, salary requirements, and a resume to jobs@medaptus.com with the title of the position as the subject line. You can also apply on LinkedIn.
