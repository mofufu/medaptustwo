MedAptus Leadership Team

EUGENE C. SCHNEIDER, PRESIDENT AND CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Mr. Schneider has direct responsibility for all MedAptus business operations. A seasoned executive with over 25 years of executive management experience in healthcare information, software and services, Mr. Schneider began working with MedAptus in 2010 in a consulting capacity. Prior to joining MedAptus he was the Chief Operating Officer at MDS, a privately-held healthcare revenue cycle services company. Mr. Schneider also served as Chief Executive Officer at HealthAnswers, a pharmaceutical industry information services provider; Senior Vice President at Ingenix (now Optum); Executive Vice President and General Manager at Medstat (now Truven Health Analytics); and as Partner at Ernst & Young, where he was the National Director for Insurance Technology Services.

Mr. Schneider holds a BS degree from Western Illinois University.


RYAN SECAN, MD, MPH, CHIEF MEDICAL OFFICER

Dr. Secan is responsible for guiding MedAptus’ communications with its physician user base as well as the clinical aspects of the company’s products and services. He is currently a practicing hospitalist at Emerson Hospital in Concord, Massachusetts. Dr. Secan has previous experience serving as medical director of hospitalist programs at two Massachusetts hospitals and has also led the deployment of mobile computing in the hospital setting. Dr. Secan holds a B.A. from the University of Rochester, and received his medical degree from the University of South Florida College of Medicine. He completed residency training in internal medicine at Wake Forest University Baptist Medical Center and later went on to obtain a Master of Public Health degree from Harvard University. Dr. Secan is board certified in internal medicine and is a member of the American College of Physicians as well as the Society of Hospital Medicine.

MICHAEL BAGLIO, CHIEF TECHNOLOGY OFFICER

Mr. Baglio is responsible for overseeing the development, quality assurance, infrastructure, and interface teams at MedAptus. Prior to MedAptus, he was the CTO and Senior Vice President of Engineering at lifeIMAGE where he oversaw the teams that built a HIPAA-compliant, SaaS multi-tenant cloud for sharing patient radiology images. Mr. Baglio also served as the Chief Architect on the Massachusetts NewMMIS project, a project dubbed by the press so technically complex that it would make “an MIT engineer sweat.” Additionally the MMIS project has been nationally recognized as a highly successful IT implementation.

Mr. Baglio has BS Degree (Magna Cum Laude) in Computer Science from U Mass – Lowell.


DAN NOTTINGHAM, VICE PRESIDENT, PRODUCT MANAGEMENT

Mr. Nottingham’s has over 20 years of experience in healthcare technology. Before coming to MedAptus to lead the company’s product management area, Dan successfully launched several new products into the healthcare market – these include ILLUMINATE for the transition of care (ABILITY Network), Cortext for secure mobile messaging (Imprivata), and Kronos Mobile for nurse scheduling/staffing (Kronos). Mr. Nottingham also spent time with McKesson Corporation and Codman Research Group where he helped to define and create healthcare analytic products based on claims data. Before launching a career in healthcare technology, Mr. Nottingham was a staff scientist at Boston University’s Center for Space Physics.


JENNIFER CROWLEY, VICE PRESIDENT, MARKETING

Ms. Crowley is responsible for the direction and execution of MedAptus’ strategic marketing initiatives. She has fifteen years of experience in healthcare and technology marketing, public relations and account management. She first started in MedAptus in 2003, working with clients on system implementation. Prior to joining MedAptus, Ms. Crowley worked in agency public relations, promoting health technology and medical device clients. She has also held a variety of positions in the non-profit healthcare sector including the Medical Outcomes Trust and the Massachusetts Department of Public Health.

Ms. Crowley received her Master’s Degree in Health Communication from the joint Emerson College/Tufts University School of Medicine program and her Bachelor’s Degree in Communication from the State University of New York at Geneseo.
