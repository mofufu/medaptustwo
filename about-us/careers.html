Careers

MedAptus solutions streamline the process of delivering patient care. From simplifying pre-care clinical workforce needs, to automating administrative tasks at the point-of-care, our offerings provide customers with a foundation for improved operational efficiency, personal productivity and ultimately, optimized financial performance. Adoption of the MedAptus suite enables providers to spend their time where it matters most: with patients.

MedAptus is always looking for motivated individuals to design, develop, sell and support our platform. If you are passionate about technology and committed to improving the experience for patients and providers alike, we want to hear from you. Our open opportunities are listed below – to express interest, please send a cover letter describing your skills, employment goals, salary requirements and a resume to:

Email: jobs@medaptus.com

Fax: 617-482-1110

Attention: Human Resources

If we don’t list a position that suits your knowledge, skills and experience, don’t be afraid to be in touch – MedAptus continues to grow and as such, our needs continue to evolve.


Current Openings


Director of Marketing

MedAptus is experiencing high product demand leading to rapid growth and is currently in search of an energetic Director of Marketing to join our team in Raleigh, NC. Founded nearly 15 years with the aim of driving improved revenue cycle performance at provider organizations, today MedAptus’ cloud and mobile technologies are revolutionizing care coordination. MedAptus’ customers include highly prestigious organizations such as MD Anderson Cancer Center, NYU Medical Center, Children’s National Medical Center, Vanderbilt University Medical Center and others.

Integration Engineer

The Integration Engineer will be responsible for complete project lifecycle of MedAptus healthcare system interfaces. This includes planning, design, development, testing, final implementation and support. Strong communication skills are required as the Integration Engineer will be responsible for project coordination, specification creation and review, as well as working with customers/vendors in identifying and solving interface challenges.
